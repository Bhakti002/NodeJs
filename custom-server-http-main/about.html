About Sports Manage

Professional sports management solutions since 2010

Our Mission

At Sports Manage, we're dedicated to transforming how sports organizations operate. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, user-friendly management solutions that empower teams, leagues, and athletes to focus on what matters most - performance and growth.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by former sports administrators who saw firsthand the challenges of managing complex sports organizations, Sports Manage started as a simple scheduling tool. Today, we've grown into a full-service sports management platform serving over 500 organizations worldwide.
